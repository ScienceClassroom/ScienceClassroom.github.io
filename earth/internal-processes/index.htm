Q&A


What evidence led to the development of the continental drift hypothesis?

If the continents in the Atlantic Ocean were puzzle pieces, they would fit together in a certain way.



What is the plate tectonics theory?

The plate tectonics theory is a theory that the lithosphere is divided into approximately two dozen "plates" that move around relative to each other, similar to ice slabs on a frozen lake.



How is mantle convection used to support the plate tectonics theory?

Some geologists believe that the main factor of of the motion of plate tectonics is the mantle's convection currents.



How do geologists study Earth’s interior?

They record the seismic waves, and study how they travel through Earth.



How does the composition and structure of each of Earth’s layers differ?

The crust is the ground that we stand on, and the lithosphere is believed to contain the tectonic plates, to name a few.
